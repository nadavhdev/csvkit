Rationale

D8 Error model & exit codes 6% Exceptional 9.0 / 10

Rationale

This is the strongest dimension. The new 0/1/2 data-condition exit code is introduced exactly as the rubric prescribes: disclosed in the epilog (csvdiff.py:345), given a dedicated "Exit codes" section in the rst, kept off the argparse-reserved 2, and asserted at exact values across every format. Crucially the author noticed that exit 1 now means "differences", so parse failures are caught and re-routed to argparser.error (exit 2) — and proved it with test_parse_error_exits_2_not_1. Column-identifier errors are caught and routed cleanly rather than leaking.
